Rice

Grilled Salmon with Rice

  • 300g Salmon
  • 200g Rice
  • 1 Lemon
  • 30ml Olive Oil

Instructions

1. Cook rice according to package instructions.
2. Grill salmon with olive oil and lemon.
3. Serve salmon over rice.

Pasta

Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 300g Ground Beef
  • 400ml Tomato Sauce
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Garlic Cloves

Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. Brown ground beef in a pan.
3. Add tomato sauce, onion, and garlic. Simmer for 10 minutes.
4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 150g Bacon
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
2. Fry bacon until crispy.
3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan cheese together.
4. Combine spaghetti, bacon, and egg mixture. Serve immediately.

Potatoes

Shepherd's Pie

  • 300g Ground Beef
  • 500g Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • 100g Peas

Instructions

1. Cook and mash potatoes.
2. Brown ground beef with carrots and peas.
3. Layer beef mixture in a dish and top with mashed potatoes.
4. Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.

Soup

Fish Chowder

  • 300g Fish Fillet
  • 200g Potatoes
  • 500ml Milk
  • 1 Onion

Instructions

1. Cook potatoes and onion in a pot.
2. Add fish and milk, then simmer for 15 minutes.
3. Serve hot.
